Event Spotlight: Forge Startup Weekend

Apply for the largest intercollegiate startup conference in the Midwest! Attendees have the opportunity to engage with VCs and seasoned industry leaders while developing their skills as an entrepreneur in workshops and mentoring sessions.

Startups will compete in the annual pitching competition and network with other top startups from UIUC, U-Chicago, Northwestern, U-Michigan. Along with a career fair and startup fair, attendees will have their resumes be included in a curated resume book sent directly to recruiters.

Startup Spotlights: 54 Startups
Campus Ventures
“Campus Ventures connects university admissions teams to high-achieving highschoolers they want but struggle to recruit—especially first-generation and underrepresented minority groups.”
iV Team:

Monica Lannom

Talia Farrell-Rosen

Jenna Mukametkhan

Charmaine Nieves

Campus Ventures is bridging the gap between underrepresented students from minority and low economic backgrounds to help prepare them for success at selective universities. They aim to be a central hub for college application resources and future career paths to promote social mobility for future generations.

Using the Technology Entrepreneur Center as a catalyst, founder Monica Lannom remembers the impact the 2020 Silicon Valley Workshop had on her entrepreneurial journey. “It was inspiring to meet with founders that were once in your shoes,” she recollected.

Campus Ventures plans to solidify their business model through deep customer discovery this summer with iVenture. By the end of the accelerator they hope to have a pilot initiative to assert the success of their program.
Objective
“Objective’s browser extension personalizes your internet to put your priorities first, adding filters and contextualized data to combat manipulative or exploitative content.”
iV Team:

Rachel Rosa

Kevin Rosa

Matthew Schroeder

Chuck Miller

Recent years have shown increasing amounts of misinformation online, specifically on social media sites. Objective wants to bring us back to organic internet content. By using online lens filters, Objective aims to personalize your browser with organic content based on your priorities and tackle this issue of misinformation.

Cofounder Rachel Rosa’s entrepreneurial journey at UIUC actually started with a different startup, QuillBot. QuillBot, as some Founders alumni will remember, is a 2018 microgrant recipient and iVenture 3 alumnus. Rachel was also a Project Manager in Illinois Enactus where she sourced and began combating social isolation of senior citizens via a storytelling platform.

Objective is excited to use the iVenture community’s expertise and mentorship to help them align their bottom line. By the end of iVenture, they hope to launch their platform and go from 0 to 1 with users.
Agriwater Tech
“Agriwater Tech’s zero-waste ionizer devices purify water while conducting real-time data analysis on water quality—helping farmers increase yield, save money and meet clean water standards.”
iV Team:

Bianca Bailey

Nicole Adair

Karolina Guerrero

Dinah Armstead

As fresh water becomes increasingly scarce, ways to purify wastewater must be explored and addressing agricultural pollution of water sources becomes critical. Agriwater Tech aims to make a global impact on these issues by providing customers with the devices and knowledge necessary to clean and maintain their own water.

Coming from a line of entrepreneurial father figures, “entrepreneurship is in [cofounder Bianca Bailey’s] DNA.” During Bianca’s undergrad at Howard University, she was president of Engineers Without Borders where she led several volunteer efforts in Kenya, Brazil, and Haiti.

Agriwater Tech looks forward to designing their prototype for a water treatment device to help farmers reduce their water pollution. They hope to use the iVenture network to go from 0 to 1 with customers.
FastTrack
“FastTrack is a community-based marketplace where musicians can collaborate with a team of music industry ninjas to refine their work and pursue their passion as their career.”
iV Team:

Josh Hoffman-Peterson

Nikhil Makhijani

Archit Gupta

Chris Brenden

FastTrack intends to make the music industry more accessible to those who are passionate about it. Anything from base lines to album art, FastTrack will facilitate the communication between you and other peer artists and professionals.

While not thinking of himself as an “entrepreneur”, founder Josh Hoffman-Peterson has always been ambitious and filled with entrepreneurial spirit. After taking several TE classes at UIUC, he was suggested by TEC professor and entrepreneur Harlee Sorkin to take the concept of FastTrack to iVenture.

FastTrack is determined to fully flush out their customer base and understand customer needs. They hope to help musicians turn their passions into stable careers.
Medius Ventures
“Medius Ventures is a media holding company that builds niche digital publications and helps its clients communicate better stories with strategic messaging and content that sells.”
iV Team:

Steven Li

Mia Amore

Lauren Beauban

Medius Ventures aims to change the game of modern media. Company incentives are based around ad revenue, not good content. Instead, Medius Ventures will concentrate their efforts on building niche publications with no display advertising.

A recipient of a Founders microgrant, founder Steven Li mentions this resource was helpful in the early days of his publication, The Rising. A campus associate, Will Robins of Sequoia and Contrary Capital, became a close friend and mentor to Steven, motivating him to continue pursuing cool ventures on campus.

Long term, Medius Ventures aims to have 5-10 leading publications concentrating on different topics. They are hoping to attain 100,000 monthly readers for their first publication and 3 University partners with which they can provide a bridge between professionals and niche majors with undersupported career development initiatives.
